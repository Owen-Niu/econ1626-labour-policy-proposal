# 5. Risks and Mitigations

The first is the risk of non-compliance by the employer with the Skills Guarantee. Phased introduction with tax credits for early adopters: provide carrots before sticks. Training quality failures will be corrected by reducing the funds provided to those who do not meet employment results. Legislative embedding will be used to prevent political reversal rather than discretionary programmes. Overestimation of the negative impact of rapid AI changes on training: The proposal does not focus on a few new-technology skills, but rather foundational digital literacy.
